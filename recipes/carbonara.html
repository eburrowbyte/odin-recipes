Carbonara alla Tabby

Description

This carbonara is a the ultimate comfort food, crafted simply from egg, cream, bacon, parmesan and your favourite pasta. You cant go wrong with this crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 200g of your prefered pasta (we like spirals)
  • 2tbs of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 250g Bacon
  • 300ml Light Thickened Cream
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 handfulls of grated parmisan (to taste)

Method

  1. Boil a pot of water and cook pasta as directed by package. Drain and set aside.
  2. Add olive oil and bacon to frying pan on med-high heat and cook until browned/crispy.
  3. Reduce heat to low and add the cream.
  4. Add one egg to the pan and mix it into the cream until incorperated, repeat with the second egg.
  5. Add parmisan one handfull at a time, mixing untill melted and incorperated into the sauce.
  6. Continue to heat on low heat until thickened.
  7. Combine pasta and creamy carbonara sauce and serve!
