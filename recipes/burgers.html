Big ol' Beefy Burgers

Description

Simple and delicious, these burger will crush your after work cravings!

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns.
  • Angus Beef Burger Patties.
  • Lettuce (cos lettuve leaves are the best!).
  • Tomato Sliced.
  • Pickle Slices.
  • Your Favourite Sauce.

Method

  2. Preheat a grill or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the Angus beef burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. While the patties are cooking, you can toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  5. Once the patties are cooked, place them on the bottom half of each toasted burger bun.
  6. Layer the lettuce leaves, tomato slices, and pickle slices on top of the patties.
  7. Optionally, you can add your favorite condiments such as ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise.
  8. Place the top half of the burger bun on top to complete the burger.
  9. Serve the burgers with your choice of sides, such as fries or a salad.
  10. Enjoy your delicious Angus beef burgers!
