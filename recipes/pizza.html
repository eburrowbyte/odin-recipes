Perfect Pizza

Description

It needs no introduction, the ultimate dinner when you just cbf.

Ingredients

  • Pizza bases (homemade or pre-made)
  • Tomato Paste
  • Ham
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Red Onion - finely diced
  • Capsicum - finely diced
  • Olives - sliced
  • Any other toppings you like!

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celcius.
  2. Spread a thin layer of tomato paste over the pizza base.
  3. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the tomato paste.
  4. Layer the ham evenly over the cheese.
  5. Drizzle a small amount of extra virgin olive oil over the pizza.
  6. Sprinkle the finely diced red onion and capsicum over the pizza.
  7. Add sliced olives and any other toppings you desire.
  8. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Once done, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute.
  10. Slice and serve hot.
  11. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
